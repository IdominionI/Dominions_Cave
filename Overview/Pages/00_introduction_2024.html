An Aether model of the Universe

Introduction

This is an outline and detailed investigation of creating a model of a universe that has space defined as an entity that is an aether. This is a project that has a purpose to document an attempt to create a hypothetical alternative model of a universe so as to gain a possible greater understanding and knowledge of the real universe that humans exist within and observe.

Therefore this aether model will have as a basis of it, the physics of the real universe, but with some alternative interpretations or lines of thought of the physics that is observed, and theories derived from. It has been discovered that many of these alternative proposals of approach to the accepted conventional thinking and methods of approach to understand and gain knowledge of the physics of the universe have previously been argued and discussed, or are at present being proposed. So this document will not repeat these proposals in detail.

No proposal of a replacement of current theory is made, but it is to express that the current thought process and accepted conventions of physics and mathematical tools and theories used to create the models of the universe may need revision and consideration of a different approach that can lead to a more clearer understanding of the universe. This then can be used to modify and enhance an understanding of the universe.

Much of modern physics to model and explain the universe seems to be very abstract and mathematical. So much so, that it could be stated that too much of modern physics that it is more like a mathematical theory than a physical description of the universe,

This document tackles the most fundamental concepts of the universe that cannot be expressed in mathematical form to be understood, but need to taken from a different angle so as to form the basis of approach from a more holistic and qualitative definition that can be applied universally.

By taking a different look and attempting to build from the ground up with different definitions of the fundamentals of the observed phenomenon of the physical universe, a different perspective of what constitutes the fundamental make up and processes of the physical universe can be derived, leading to a greater approach to understanding and comprehension of what is observed.

Such fundamental concepts that forms the basis of all of physics such as space, time and energy are tackled to give what may be different definitions, interpretations and explanations as to what they are and how they fit in with the universe. These will not break with current physics, but to offer an alternative perspective of what they may be.

Other interpretations and explanations of physical phenomenon will be presented with arguments that may involve some mathematical treatment to help explain such an argument. But as has been discovered, many a consideration has already been presented in the past that correlates with what is proposed in this document by past and present minds that have put forward the same arguments with greater detail, treatment and explanation. It is comforting to find that when one comes up with what one considers as an original thought, or approach of thinking, to then find out that others have also come up with the same.

So it can be forgiven if there are such arguments that have been proposed here that have already been documented and better presented, but not known by the author.

What is important is to consider that the current understanding of the universe as proposed and modelled by physicists can be flawed by a following bias of conventional thought. This is not uncommon in history. Many a greater understanding and modelling of the physical universe, and other scientific disciplines have been thawted by conventional authorities chained down by not accepting new or different trains of thinking. This has either been because of personal traits related to ego, reputation, status and belief systems, or by institutions unable to accept alternative positions for the same and other reasons. But this is not all together a bad thing. To change the thought processes and acceptance of new theories of understanding require strong arguments with evidence to back them up.

This document is not an attempt to change or challenge such current conventions or authorities of physics, just to offer a different approach to thinking to gain a greater understanding of the universe and its workings. The author hopes this document will be helpful in that respect. It is also a document of personal endeavour so as to record personal trains of thought so as to act as a kind of notebook for future reference and not to forget or repeat ones thoughts. As such the documentation may have poor structure or large dialogue of thoughts in an attempt to explain certain physical concepts or processes etc. The Strange aspect of this is that as further investigations proceed, many of these personal thoughts or aspects of the workings of the universe are discovered to be part of past or current physics, but with certain differences in perspective, which is what this document is all about.
